National Light Switch Appreciation Association

Basic Light Switch

An excellent utility light switch with long standing durability and an immense ease of use. Only downside is its protrusion may cause unintentional injuries if pressed upon incorrectly.

9/10

Rocker Light Switch

Still rather elegant in practicality, and a worthy purchase for those who find more satisfaction in a button than a lever. However, it can be much harder to tell whether the switch is in the "on" or "off" position from a distance.

7/10

Slide Switch

These switches may differ whether sliding the switch doubles as a dimmer, or if it's a simple on/off switch. Either way, it causes undue difficulties in turning the light all the way on or all the way off on a whim.

5/10

Dimmer Slide Switch

This version comes pre-equipped with a more typical switch coupled with the dimmer slide. Very nice addition that fixes the major gripe with the previous iteration, creating possibly the strongest switch on the market.

10/10

Motion Sensor Switch

An abomination.

0/10
