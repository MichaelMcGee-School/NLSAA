Behind every light is an even greater light switch.

Here at the NLSAA we believe that light switches deserve far more recognition than they currently get. We would like to start by thanking the great men and women who have contributed to the development of this great creation. For without it, our lights would either be on or off at all moments, forever, either constantly wasting power or being pathetically useless.

Eliminating the darkness in our world when we really need it.

Light switches allow us to illuminate the eternal night that consumes all enclosures at a simple flick of the wrist. The invention of the light switch has contributed to the lessening of personal injuries in dark spaces. However, it leaves the option to keep the lights off either during the day, or when we would rather go to sleep, where personal injuries are significantly less likely.
