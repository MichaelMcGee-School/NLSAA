Light Switches Can Be Very Complicated

Different types of switches take different ranges of motion to turn them on and off. Knowing what type of light switch you are using can affect effectiveness of your flicks.

General Technique

  • Standard Switch
    1. Pinch the switch between your thumb and index finger.
    2. To turn the lights on, push up on the switch.
    3. To turn the lights off, push down on the switch.
    4. Instructions may vary if the lights are connected to multiple switches.
  • Rocker Switches
    1. Push in on the extended portion of the switch to release electricity to the lights.
    2. If desired illumination is not reached, press in on the other side of the switch.
  • Dimmer Switch
    1. Press in on the switch to turn the lights on.
    2. Twist the switch clockwise to brighten the lights.
    3. Twist the switch counter-clockwise to dim the lights.
    4. Push in on the switch again to turn the lights off.
